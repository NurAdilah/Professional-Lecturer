PROFILE

Dr. Norizan Anwar is a senior lecturer and researcher since 12 years ago. Dr. Norizan Anwar's higher qualification is in Doctor of Philosophy (Information Management)(PhD), Universiti Teknologi MARA, Cawangan Shah Alam. Currently she beholding a permanent position as senior lecturer in the Department Faculty of Information Management, Universiti Teknologi MARA. Her field of specific expertise is Management of Information Systems, Information Management Systems Design and Implementation Assessment of Technology Effectiveness. Dr. Norizan's research interests are related to information systems resources, information services and school resource center and also information privacy. With her experience, Dr. Norizan has been appointed as consultation in doing the paper work.
